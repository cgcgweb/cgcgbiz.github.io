Selected Agglomeration

A brief overview of select opportunities we were fortunate enough to be able to participate in.

Construction & Design firm

L

Business size

The client is a large-sized modular construction and design firm with offices in three states, and employees throughout the world who work remotely and have a large manufacturing plant.

Issues

Cybersecurity

Lackluster technical team

Handing over managing of IT services to CGCGC will ensure you a high efficiency and timely improvement of your IT environment and processes within it.

No proactive cybersecurity measures in place.

Proposed Solution

Make a change to their IT structure and support.

We took on the management of all their IT functions in their Manhattan office and provided co-managed support with the client's Technology Department in their office/manufacturing plant, acting as a Tier IV Helpdesk.
We offered a range of endpoint protection, network upgrading and generally consulted on ways to make their network better connected for a smoother workflow. We also put together a cloud-based backup plan in the case of a disaster.

Results

New network setup

Next-generation Firewall

New products for better team interactions

Multi-layered system to prevent spam

After onboarding, the client was set up with a new network, next-generation firewall, remote management, and endpoint detection software. When phishing emails spiked, we deployed a multi-layered system to prevent spam, spoofing, and other business email compromise attacks. In addition, we began to manage all aspects of the IT structure and implemented new products that allowed for better team interaction and connectivity.

Community Center

M

Business size

The client is a popular community center focused on providing an educational outlet through artistic expression for people of all ages.

Issues

Recently, they were given the opportunity to move into a brand-new space as part of a revitalization initiative. The client approached Cyber Guardian Consulting Group (CGCG) to plan and install the IT infrastructure, requesting state-of-the-art technology to accompany their new space.

 

Proposed Solution

Developing a cutting-edge network plan

Based on what our client was looking for, we developed a plan encapsulating everything from door access control and cloud managed cameras to endpoint protection and a next generation firewall. 
We were able to eliminate redundant electrical runs by incorporating power over ethernet wherever possible.

 

Building IT Structure

Working with the architect, electrician, general contractor and other groups, we helped to adapt the building plans in order to meet our client’s IT needs and desires.

 

Results

New network setup

Next-generation Firewall

Stayed within budget

Brought access drops to each location

Eliminated redundant electrical runs

expanded the ethernet to all locations

From the beginning, the client was satisfied with our ability to be flexible and work closely with their team in order to stick to their projected completion time when change orders were submitted. We were able to adapt quickly and accordingly.
Additionally, we helped them stay within budget while actualizing their vision for a next-generation experience.
The client was impressed with our ability to coordinate between teams by making sure that the project was moving forward at a pace in line with and then exceeding their expectations. Throughout this project we have developed lasting relationships with all the teams involved and are excited to take on more projects within the non-profit and community-support sectors.
