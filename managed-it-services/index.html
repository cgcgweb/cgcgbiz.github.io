Cloud-to-Cloud

The practice of copying data stored on one cloud service to another cloud service. The backups are stored on the second cloud service, serving the same purpose as an off-site, air gapped backup.

The objective of a DR plan is to ensure that an organization can respond to a disaster or other emergency that affects information systems – and minimize the effect on business operations.
