CISO Advisory Services

We will be responsible for developing and implementing an information security program, which includes procedures and policies designed to protect enterprise communications, systems and assets from both internal and external threats.

Why do you need a CISO?

Most businesses don’t have the resources to employ a full-time Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), and often don’t know exactly how to articulate the needs and responsibilities a CISO would undertake. Some companies may have an existing CISO who needs the guidance of a senior expert to improve their security or compliance. By working with a CISO from Cyber Guardian we can provide your organisation with a cost-effective way of maintaining information security systems and managing risk and improve the overall security of your systems, networks, infrastructures, and applications.

Evaluate

We perform an in-depth analysis of the entire information security program for your organization, as well as test the infrastructure as needed.

Design

We create a personalized risk-based approach to information security featuring practicical and effecitive security measures.

Maintain

We monitor the processes and periodically review all metrics and oversight reporting to provide suggestions for improvement.

Implement

We implement threat detection and monitoring solutions as well as integrate services delivered by third parties into seamless and secure framework.
